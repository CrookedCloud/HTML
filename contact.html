Haukai Restaurant, located in the beautiful Bay of Islands, is a celebration of Māori kai and culture. the restaurant aims to offer an authentic dining experience. With a deep connection to Te Ao Māori, our owners work full-time to create a welcoming environment that blends traditional Māori cuisine with Pākehā options, offering a diverse and exciting menu. At Haukai, we believe in the importance of cultural heritage, which is why we incorporate Māori practices such as karakia, a blessing of the food, into our dining experience. This connection to Māori culture enriches every visit, providing not just a meal but a journey into the traditions of Aotearoa. Currently open in the evenings from Wednesday to Sunday. Whether you're craving traditional Māori kai or modern Pākehā dishes, our menu is designed to offer something for everyone. We invite you to dine with us, immerse yourself in Māori culture, and enjoy a unique and memorable experience.
